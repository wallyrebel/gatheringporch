Our Story

Where Every Moment
Deserves to Be Celebrated

At The Gathering Porch, we believe that life's most meaningful moments deserve a setting as special as the occasion itself. Nestled in the heart of Ripley, Mississippi, our venue offers a warm, inviting atmosphere where families and friends come together to create memories that last a lifetime.

Let our venue and catering staff help celebrate this fun season of your life. Whether you're planning an intimate bridal shower or a grand celebration, our dedicated team — led by Misty — brings passion, creativity, and meticulous attention to detail to every event we host.
